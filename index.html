Who Am I?

Hello World!

My name is Elizabeth Havird, and I am a full-stack web developer.

I offer web-design & developer services to the Los Angeles area, the internet, and beyond!

It was the year 2001. I was 4 years old. My mother came home with a brand new laptop computer.
"This means you can use my old one.", she said, pointing to her Windows 98. Since that day, my affinity for technology has only grown stronger. The more I learned, the more I realized, there will always be so much more to learn! This persistant inclination eventually lead me to Learn Academy, a 580 hour, intensive, 4 month bootcamp for up and coming developers.

I graduated in May 2020 and am currently seeking employment.
